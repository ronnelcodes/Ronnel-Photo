My Journey into Coding

Posted on January 1, 2017 at 12:00 PM

Where do I begin? How do I start my story? Do I go into an elaborate detail of my career, starting from being a basic gopher at Wal-Mart? Do I talk more about the technical jobs I had. I could. But I won't. Reason being is that is the past

What I will do is this: talk about my love of code. I became aware of my love in May of 2017. I work in IT, but found a video on YouTube about coding and web development and teaching yourself. I thought about this as a side hustle type of situation.

What I didn't know is that I would fall in love with coding.

Ever since May, I have been pushing myself to learn every aspect of web development. I went through a program called Free Code Camp and got up to the last two projects before I got a free certificate and didn't finish it.

There was nothing wrong with me or the program itself, but I wanted to apporach my learning differently. Then I saw a video by Chris Sean.

This was it. This was the change I was looking for. This was the push that started me on my journey.

Since then, I have been running full speed, making websites and learning as much as I can so I can get my first job as a junior web developer. Stay tuned
